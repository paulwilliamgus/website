Horne Lake Campgrounds

About:

Campgrounds are located at the west end of Horne Lake. They are beside the Horne Lake Caves.

  • 5km of lake & river frontto explore
  • Boat ramp
  • Canoe rentals
  • Forest trails

Facilities:

  • Out houses located on site

Prices:

  • $10-$40/night
  • Additional fees
    • Charge for launching boat
    • 50% extra for other vehicles

Group sites:

  • April 1st-October 31st
    • Minimum 15 people
    • $5 per person
  • May 1st-September 30th
    • Minimum 15 people
    • $10 per person

Things to do:

  • Historical Horne Lake trail
  • Horne Lake
    • Guides can lead visiters through caves
    • Two small caves visitors can explore on own
    • guided tour of larger cave
