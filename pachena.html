Vancouver Island Campgrounds

Pachena Campgrounds

About:

The campground is located at the mouth of Pachena river next to Huu-ay-aht First Nations community of Anacla. It is next to sandy beaches facing the open pacific ocean.

  • Room for RVs, campers, tents
  • 5 km from Bamfield
  • Malist Public House in Bamfield(restaurant)
  • Gas bar
  • Head of West Coast Trail

Facilities:

  • Two facilities
  • Hot showers
  • Flush toilets

Tent sites:

  • 8 oceanview sites
  • 50 tent sites (various sizes)
    • $119/night
    • 16ftx20ft
    • 4 cots
    • Table
    • Fire pit
    • Woodstove

Prices:

  • Range from $39-$69
  • Paddle board rentals ($25/halfday or $50/full day)

Activities:

  • West Coast Trail (Opens May 1st-September 30th)
  • Sport fishing/charter packages
  • Whale watching
  • Kiixin tours (Historical site)
